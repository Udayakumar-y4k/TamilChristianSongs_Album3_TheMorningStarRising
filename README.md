# TamilChristianSongs_Album3_TheMorningStarRising
The Morning Star Rising - காலை நட்சத்திரம் உதயமாகுதல்
Revelation 22:16 – “I, Jesus… am the Root and the Offspring of David, the Bright and Morning Star.”
2 Peter 1:19 – “…until the day dawns and the morning star rises in your hearts.”
This album will be a dawn chorus — songs of hope, awakening, new light, and the soon-coming return of the Bright Morning Star.
